Detailed Specifications

Crafted with precision, Urban Square Apartments provide top-notch materials and finishes that ensure durability and elegance.

  • Designer Tile DADO: 2 feet height over the kitchen counter and 4 feet height in the utility area.
  • Main Door: Wooden door frame with a 40mm thick teak wood veneer shutter, polished on both sides.
  • Plumbing & Sanitary Fittings: CPVC concealed piping with luxury CP fittings and sanitaryware from Kohler, Roca, or equivalent.
  • Elevator: 10-passenger capacity elevator (Johnson or equivalent) for smooth vertical transport.
  • Power Backup: Supports 1 light and 1 fan in living, dining, and all bedrooms with a 2.5 KVA load limiter, plus backup for the elevator and common lighting.
  • Electrical: Concealed wiring with modular switches (Havells Crabtree or equivalent), 3-phase supply with MCB & RCCB, telephone point in the living and master bedroom, and A/C provisions for all bedrooms, living, and dining.
